<p><p>An Optical Parametric Oscillator (OPO) is a device that generates coherent light through the non-linear interaction of a pump laser in a nonlinear crystal, producing two wavelengths: the signal and idler. The OPO market can be segmented into single resonance and double resonance types. Single resonance OPOs enhance one of the output wavelengths, while double resonance OPOs amplify both wavelengths. Each type serves various applications, including telecommunications, spectroscopy, and medical imaging, catering to different performance and sensitivity requirements.</p></p>
