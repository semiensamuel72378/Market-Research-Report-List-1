<p><p>CNC precision vises are essential in machining operations, categorized into single station and multi-station types. Single station CNC precision vises are designed for securing one workpiece at a time, offering high accuracy for tasks requiring specific alignment. In contrast, multi-station CNC precision vises can hold multiple workpieces simultaneously, enhancing productivity and efficiency in batch processing. The choice between these types depends on production volume, complexity of machining tasks, and the need for speed versus precision.</p></p>
