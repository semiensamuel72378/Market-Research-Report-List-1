<p><p>Chiral column chromatography is essential for separating enantiomers in pharmaceuticals and chemical compounds. The market comprises two main types: gas chromatography and liquid chromatography systems. Gas chromatography utilizes vaporized samples for separation and is ideal for volatile compounds. In contrast, liquid chromatography employs a liquid mobile phase to separate non-volatile and larger molecules, making it suitable for a broader range of applications. Both systems are critical for ensuring purity and efficacy in chiral molecule analysis and drug development.</p></p>
