<p><p>The tissue sealants and adhesives market is divided into two main types: tissue adhesives and tissue sealants. Tissue adhesives are synthetic or natural substances used to bond skin and tissue, offering quick closure with minimal scarring. They are often favored for their ease of use and reduced infection risk. Tissue sealants, on the other hand, are used to prevent or control bleeding by sealing tissues during surgical procedures. Both products enhance wound healing and reduce complications, making them vital in modern surgical practices.</p></p>
