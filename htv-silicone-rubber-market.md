<p><p>The HTV silicone rubber market encompasses various processing methods: **Compression Molding** involves shaping rubber under heat and pressure, ideal for large, durable parts. **Extrusion** creates continuous profiles like seals and tubes by forcing rubber through a die. **Injection Molding** uses high pressure to inject rubber into molds, suitable for complex, high-precision components. **Other methods** include calendering or liquid silicone rubber (LSR) molding, catering to specialized applications. Each method offers unique advantages in terms of efficiency, precision, and scalability for diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
