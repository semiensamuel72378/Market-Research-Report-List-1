<p><ul><li>Organic</li><li>Inorganic</li><li>Resin</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The welding flux market is categorized into three main types: organic, inorganic, and resin-based fluxes. Organic fluxes are derived from natural materials and offer good cleaning properties, making them suitable for certain welding processes. Inorganic fluxes, often mineral-based, provide excellent thermal stability and are commonly used in high-temperature applications. Resin fluxes, formulated from synthetic polymers, improve adhesion and moisture resistance, enhancing the overall quality of welds. Each type serves specific applications and contributes to the overall efficiency of welding processes.</p></p>
