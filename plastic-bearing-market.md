<p><ul><li>Plastic Rolling Bearings</li><li>Plastic Sliding Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The plastic bearing market comprises two main types: plastic rolling bearings and plastic sliding bearings. Plastic rolling bearings utilize rolling elements to minimize friction, offering lightweight and corrosion-resistant solutions for various applications, particularly in environments where metal bearings would be unsuitable. On the other hand, plastic sliding bearings operate through direct surface contact between moving parts, providing a low-friction alternative ideal for applications requiring durability without lubrication. Both types cater to diverse industrial applications, enhancing efficiency and reducing maintenance needs.</p></p>
