<p><p>The Sapphire Spherical Lenses market comprises three main types: concave, convex, and planoconvex lenses. Concave lenses are used to diverge light, ideal for applications like corrective eyewear and optical instruments. Convex lenses, on the other hand, converge light, making them suitable for focusing applications in cameras and projectors. Planoconvex lenses feature one flat and one curved surface, providing versatility in light manipulation, often used in laser systems and optical devices. Each type serves distinct roles across various industries, enhancing optical performance.</p></p>
