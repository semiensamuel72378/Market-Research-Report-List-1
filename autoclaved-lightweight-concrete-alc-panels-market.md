<p><p>Autoclaved Lightweight Concrete (ALC) panels are made using materials like silica sand and fly ash, which play a crucial role in their production. Silica sand provides the required aggregate, contributing to the panels' strength and durability. Fly ash, a byproduct of coal combustion, enhances workability and reduces environmental impact by recycling waste. Both materials improve the thermal and acoustic properties of ALC panels, making them an attractive choice for construction in energy-efficient and sustainable building projects.</p></p>
