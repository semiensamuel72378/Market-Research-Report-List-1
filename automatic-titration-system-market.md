<p><strong>The Automatic Titration System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Coulometric Titration</li><li>Volumetric Titration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Titration System market encompasses two primary types: coulometric titration and volumetric titration. Coulometric titration measures the amount of reagent needed to reach the endpoint by determining the electrical charge required to convert the analyte completely. In contrast, volumetric titration involves the precise measurement of liquid reagent volume needed to achieve the endpoint, typically indicated by a color change. Both methods are essential in various industries, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverage, and environmental testing, for accurate quantitative analysis.</p></p>
