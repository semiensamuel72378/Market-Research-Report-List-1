<p><ul><li>Urea Breath Detection Kit</li><li>Fecal Detection Kit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Gastric ulcer detection kits include Urea Breath Detection Kits and Fecal Detection Kits, which help diagnose Helicobacter pylori infection, a major cause of gastric ulcers. The Urea Breath Detection Kit measures carbon dioxide in the breath after ingesting a urea solution, indicating the presence of the bacteria. In contrast, the Fecal Detection Kit analyzes stool samples for H. pylori antigens. Both methods are non-invasive, providing healthcare providers with reliable tools to detect gastric ulcers and implement appropriate treatments.</p></p>
