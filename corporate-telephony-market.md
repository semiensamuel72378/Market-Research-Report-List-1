Key Telephone Systems are traditional setups featuring physical keysets that allow users to manage calls directly, ideal for smaller businesses. Wireless Private Branch Exchange systems enable mobility by providing wireless phone connectivity, enhancing communication within dynamic environments, and catering to the growing need for flexible workplace solutions.</p></p>
