<p><p>The New Energy Vehicles (NEV) Brake-by-Wire (BBW) systems market includes Two-Box and One-Box configurations. Two-Box systems separate the brake control unit and hydraulic unit, offering redundancy and flexibility, ideal for high-performance or safety-critical applications. One-Box systems integrate both units into a single module, reducing weight, cost, and complexity, making them suitable for mass-market NEVs. Both systems enhance energy recovery and efficiency, aligning with NEV goals, but differ in design, cost, and application suitability.</p></p>
