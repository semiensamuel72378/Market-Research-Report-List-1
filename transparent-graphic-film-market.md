<p><p>The Transparent Graphic Film market is categorized mainly into four types: PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), PP (Polypropylene), PE (Polyethylene), and Others. PVC films are known for their durability and versatility, making them popular for various applications. PP films are lightweight and resilient, ideal for packaging and labels. PE films offer excellent moisture barrier properties, suitable for food packaging. The "Others" category includes alternative materials like PET (Polyethylene Terephthalate) and biodegradable options, catering to specialized or eco-friendly needs in graphic applications.</p></p>
