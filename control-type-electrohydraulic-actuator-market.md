<p><p>The control type electrohydraulic actuator market is categorized into linear and rotary actuators. Linear actuators convert hydraulic pressure into straight-line motion, making them ideal for applications like lifts, gates, and positioning systems. On the other hand, rotary actuators utilize hydraulic power to produce rotational movement, suitable for tasks such as valve operation and robotic arms. Both actuator types are widely used in industries such as manufacturing, aerospace, and automotive, driven by the demand for precision and efficiency in automation processes.</p></p>
