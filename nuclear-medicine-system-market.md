<p><p>The Nuclear Medicine System market encompasses various imaging technologies used for diagnosing and treating diseases. Single-Photon Emission Computed Tomography (SPECT) systems provide three-dimensional images by detecting gamma rays from radiotracers. Hybrid Positron Emission Tomography (PET) systems combine PET and CT imaging, enhancing localization and anatomical detail. Planar scintigraphy involves two-dimensional imaging to assess organ function and detect abnormalities. Each type offers unique benefits, catering to different diagnostic needs in the healthcare sector.</p></p>
