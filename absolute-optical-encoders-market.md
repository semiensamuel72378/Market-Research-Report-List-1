<p><strong>The Absolute Optical Encoders Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-turn Encoder</li><li>Multi-turn Encoder</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The absolute optical encoders market is categorized into single-turn and multi-turn encoders. Single-turn encoders provide position data within a single revolution, making them suitable for applications requiring limited positional accuracy. In contrast, multi-turn encoders track rotational position over multiple revolutions, using gears or batteries to maintain position during power loss. Together, these encoder types cater to diverse applications, including robotics, automation, and industrial machinery, by offering precise and reliable positional feedback across varying operational conditions.</p></p>
