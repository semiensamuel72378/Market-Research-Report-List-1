<p><p>WPC (Wood Plastic Composite) flooring encompasses various types, primarily differentiated by their plastic components: PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), PE (Polyethylene), and PP (Polypropylene). PVC WPC flooring is known for its durability and resistance to moisture, making it ideal for high-humidity environments. PE flooring offers enhanced flexibility and is lightweight, suitable for both indoor and outdoor applications. PP flooring, while less common, is recognized for its sturdiness and chemical resistance, providing a robust option in versatile settings. Together, they cater to diverse market needs.</p></p>
