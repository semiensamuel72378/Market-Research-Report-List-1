<p><p>The titanium dioxide market is primarily categorized into three types: Anatase, Rutile, and Other types. Anatase is favored for its high dispersion and ease of processing, making it suitable for applications in plastics and coatings. Rutile, being more robust and lightfast, is preferred in high-performance coatings and pigments. The "Other" category includes specialty grades that cater to niche applications, such as photocatalysts and electronic products. Together, these types serve diverse industries, including construction, automotive, and consumer goods.</p></p>
