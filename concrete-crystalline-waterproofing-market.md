<p><strong>The Concrete Crystalline Waterproofing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integral Crystalline Waterproofing</li><li>Topical Crystalline Waterproofing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Concrete Crystalline Waterproofing market consists of two primary types: Integral Crystalline Waterproofing and Topical Crystalline Waterproofing. Integral Crystalline Waterproofing involves adding waterproofing compounds directly to the concrete mix, enhancing protection from water penetration throughout the material. In contrast, Topical Crystalline Waterproofing is applied as a surface treatment, forming a barrier that reacts with moisture to produce a crystalline structure that blocks water intrusion. Both methods are effective in protecting structures from water damage and improving durability.</p></p>
