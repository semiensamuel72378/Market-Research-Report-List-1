<p><p>The two-wheeler insurance market primarily consists of two main types: Third Party Insurance and Comprehensive Insurance. Third Party Insurance covers legal liabilities to third parties for injuries or damages caused by the insured vehicle, making it mandatory in many regions. In contrast, Comprehensive Insurance offers broader coverage, including damage to the insured vehicle and theft, along with third-party liabilities. Additionally, the distributor market plays a vital role, comprising agents, brokers, and online platforms that facilitate insurance sales and services to customers.</p></p>
