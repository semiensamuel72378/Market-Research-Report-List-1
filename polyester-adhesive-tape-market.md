<p><p>The Polyester Adhesive Tape market is divided into two main types: simple-face tape and double-sided tape. Simple-face tape features adhesive on one side, ideal for securing, sealing, or masking applications. It offers ease of use and versatility in various environments. In contrast, double-sided tape has adhesive on both sides, allowing for effective bonding between surfaces without visible tape, making it suitable for mounting, insulation, and crafting. Both types cater to different industrial and consumer needs while enhancing efficiency and durability.</p></p>
