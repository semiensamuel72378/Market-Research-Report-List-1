<p><p>Automated pipetting systems are categorized into two main types: multi-channel and single-channel. Multi-channel pipetting systems enable simultaneous dispensing of liquids across multiple wells, significantly enhancing throughput and efficiency in high-throughput applications, such as drug screening and genomics. In contrast, single-channel pipetting systems are designed for precise, flexible liquid handling in various applications, offering greater accuracy for tasks that require specific volumes. Each type serves distinct needs within laboratories, balancing speed and precision according to workflow requirements.</p></p>
