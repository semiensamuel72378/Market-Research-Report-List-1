<p><p>The car ignition system market includes types such as spark plugs and ignition coils, which are essential for engine performance. Spark plugs ignite the air-fuel mixture in the combustion chamber using electrical sparks, ensuring efficient combustion. Ignition coils, on the other hand, convert the battery's low voltage into high voltage needed to create those sparks. Together, they play a critical role in starting the engine and optimizing its performance, affecting fuel efficiency and emissions in vehicles.</p></p>
