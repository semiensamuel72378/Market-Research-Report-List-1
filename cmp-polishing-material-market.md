<p><ul><li>CMP Slurry</li><li>CMP Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP polishing material market comprises two main types: CMP slurry and CMP pads. CMP slurry consists of abrasive particles suspended in a liquid, designed for chemical-mechanical polishing processes to achieve desired surface finishes on semiconductor wafers and other materials. CMP pads, on the other hand, provide the necessary surface for the wafer to contact during polishing, facilitating uniformity and control. Together, these components play a crucial role in the semiconductor manufacturing process, ensuring precision and performance in chip production.</p></p>
