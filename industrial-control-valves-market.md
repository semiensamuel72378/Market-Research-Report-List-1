<p><p>The industrial control valves market primarily features two main types: 2-way and 3-way valves. 2-way valves are designed to regulate flow by either allowing or blocking it, ideal for on/off control in various applications. In contrast, 3-way valves manage the flow between three ports, enabling mixing or diverting processes. This functionality makes them essential for applications requiring precise flow control or temperature regulation. Both types are crucial for enhancing efficiency in industrial operations across various sectors.</p></p>
