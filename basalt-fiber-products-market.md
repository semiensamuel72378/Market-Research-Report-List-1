<p><p>The basalt fiber products market is primarily divided into two types: basalt chopped fiber and basalt continuous fiber. Basalt chopped fiber consists of short strands used in applications requiring reinforcement in composites, enhancing strength and thermal resistance in construction and automotive sectors. In contrast, basalt continuous fiber is produced as unbroken strands offering superior tensile strength, ideal for applications in aerospace, marine, and industrial sectors. Both types leverage basalt's natural properties, contributing to lightweight and durable material solutions.</p></p>
