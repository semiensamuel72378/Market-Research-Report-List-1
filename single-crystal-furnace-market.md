<p><strong>The Single Crystal Furnace Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Czochralski (CZ) Method Furnace</li><li>Floating Zone (FZ) Method Furnace</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The single crystal furnace market is primarily categorized into two types: Czochralski (CZ) method furnaces and Floating Zone (FZ) method furnaces. CZ method furnaces are widely used for growing semiconductor crystals, particularly silicon, by pulling a single crystal from a molten state. In contrast, FZ method furnaces utilize a non-contact technique where a feed rod is melted in a localized zone, allowing for high-purity crystal growth without contamination, making it ideal for specialized materials like sapphire and silicon carbide.</p></p>
