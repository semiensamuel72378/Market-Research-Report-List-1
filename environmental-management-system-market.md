<p><p>Environmental Management Systems (EMS) encompass various market types, primarily ISO 14001 and the European Union's EMAS. ISO 14001 provides a framework for organizations to enhance their environmental performance through systematic management practices. It’s widely adopted globally, facilitating compliance and continuous improvement. EMAS, on the other hand, is more stringent, requiring organizations to publish an environmental statement and undergo periodic audits. Both systems aim to promote sustainable practices, reduce environmental impact, and demonstrate commitment to environmental stewardship.</p></p>
