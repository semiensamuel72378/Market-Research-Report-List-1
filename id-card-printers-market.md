<p><p>The ID card printers market primarily includes two types: Dye Sublimation (DyeSub) printers and Inkjet printers. DyeSub printers use heat to transfer dye onto the card surface, producing vibrant, durable images ideal for high-volume production. They are commonly used for ID cards, badges, and membership cards. In contrast, Inkjet printers utilize liquid ink to print images on cards, offering versatility for custom designs and lower upfront costs, making them suitable for smaller-scale or specialized applications.</p></p>
