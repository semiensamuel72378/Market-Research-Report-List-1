<p><p>The coated art paper market comprises two main types: Coated One Side (C1S) and Coated Two Side (C2S). C1S paper features a glossy, coated finish on one side, making it ideal for applications requiring high-quality images, such as brochures and displays. In contrast, C2S paper has a coated finish on both sides, offering enhanced print quality and vibrant color reproduction, suitable for magazines, catalogs, and promotional materials. Both types serve distinct needs in the printing industry, catering to various design requirements and applications.</p></p>
