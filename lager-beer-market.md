<p><p>Lager beer comprises two main market types: Pasteurized and Draft beer. Conversely, Draft beer is served fresh from kegs, often in bars or restaurants, providing a unique flavor profile due to its minimal processing. Both types cater to different consumer preferences, with Pasteurized options prioritizing longevity and convenience, while Draft offers a fresher taste experience and is typically favored by craft beer enthusiasts.</p></p>
