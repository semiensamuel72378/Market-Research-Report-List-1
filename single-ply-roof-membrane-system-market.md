<p><p>The Single-Ply Roof Membrane System market is categorized into two main types: Thermoset Roof Membrane Systems and Thermoplastic Roof Membrane Systems. Thermoset membranes, such as EPDM, are characterized by their high durability and resistance to UV rays and chemicals; they are cured during production. In contrast, Thermoplastic membranes, like PVC and TPO, are heat-weldable, allowing for flexible installation and repair. Both systems offer energy efficiency and long-lasting protection, catering to diverse roofing needs across commercial and residential applications.</p></p>
