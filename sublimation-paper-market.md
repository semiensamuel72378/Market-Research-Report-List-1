<p><ul><li>Laser Printing</li><li>Inkjet Printing</li><li>Dye-sublimation Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sublimation paper market comprises three main types based on printing methods: laser printing, inkjet printing, and dye-sublimation printing. Laser printing utilizes heat to transfer sublimation ink onto the paper, creating vibrant images. Inkjet printing employs liquid ink and specialized sublimation inks, allowing for detailed designs. Dye-sublimation printing directly converts solid ink into gas, bonding it with substrates such as fabrics and ceramics, resulting in long-lasting and vivid prints. Each method caters to specific applications within the sublimation process.</p></p>
