<p><p>The food sterilizer market comprises several types, each utilizing distinct methods for food preservation. Heat sterilization involves high temperatures to kill microorganisms, while steam sterilization uses pressurized steam for similar purposes. Radiation sterilization employs ionizing radiation to eliminate pathogens, and chemical sterilization incorporates substances like chlorine or hydrogen peroxide. Filtration sterilization removes contaminants through physical barriers like membranes. Additionally, "Others" includes emerging and alternative methods, catering to diverse food safety needs and preferences in the industry.</p></p>
