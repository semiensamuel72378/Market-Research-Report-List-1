<p><strong>The Benchtop Water Analysis Instrumentation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Physical Assay</li><li>Chemical Assay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The benchtop water analysis instrumentation market comprises two main types: physical assay and chemical assay. Physical assays involve the measurement of water's physical properties, such as temperature, turbidity, and conductivity, to assess its quality. In contrast, chemical assays focus on analyzing the chemical composition of water, detecting contaminants and pollutants through techniques like titration and spectrometry. Both types are crucial for ensuring water safety and compliance with environmental regulations, serving industries like research, municipal water treatment, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
