<p><p>Discrete manufacturing ERP market comprises two primary types: on-premise ERP and cloud-based ERP. On-premise ERP solutions are installed locally on a company's servers and offer greater control and customization but require significant upfront costs and maintenance. Conversely, cloud-based ERP systems are hosted on remote servers, allowing for easier access, scalability, and lower initial investment. They leverage subscription pricing models, enabling manufacturers to manage operations efficiently while minimizing IT overhead and facilitating real-time data access and collaboration.</p></p>
