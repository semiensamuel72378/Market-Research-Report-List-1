<p><p>The gas masks market comprises various types designed for different protection needs. Disposable respirators are single-use, providing basic filtration for dust and particulates. Air Purifying Respirators (APRs) use filters to remove contaminants from the air. Powered Air Purifying Respirators (PAPRs) enhance airflow with a battery-operated fan. Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBAs) supply breathable air from a tank, ideal for toxic environments. Other market types may include specialized masks for industrial, military, or emergency services applications, catering to diverse user requirements.</p></p>
