<p><p>Disconnectors are crucial components in electrical systems that isolate circuits to ensure safety during maintenance. The Centre-break Disconnector features a single pivot point in the middle, allowing for efficient operation and compact design, making it suitable for limited spaces. In contrast, Double-break Disconnectors have two pivot points, providing improved electrical performance and greater isolation capability, often used in high-voltage applications. Both types are essential for maintaining the integrity and safety of electrical networks.</p></p>
