<p><strong>The PET Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>BoPET</li><li>CPET</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PET film market includes BoPET (Biaxially Oriented Polyethylene Terephthalate) and CPET (Crystalline Polyethylene Terephthalate). BoPET films are widely used for packaging, electronics, and imaging due to their high strength, transparency, and thermal stability. CPET films, known for their crystallinity, are ideal for rigid food containers and trays, offering excellent heat resistance and durability. Both types cater to diverse industries, with BoPET focusing on flexible applications and CPET on rigid, heat-resistant solutions, reflecting the versatility of PET-based materials in modern manufacturing.</p></p>
