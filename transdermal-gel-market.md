<p><strong>The Transdermal Gel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ionophoresis</li><li>Electroporation</li><li>Sonophoresis</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The transdermal gel market encompasses various technologies to enhance drug delivery through the skin. Ionophoresis utilizes electrical currents to drive charged drugs into the skin, while electroporation applies short voltage pulses to create temporary pores in cell membranes, facilitating deeper drug penetration. Sonophoresis employs ultrasound waves to increase skin permeability, allowing larger molecules to enter. These methods enhance bioavailability and therapeutic efficacy, making transdermal gels a viable option for systemic drug delivery across various medical applications.</p></p>
