<p><p>The digital meter market comprises two primary types: stationary and portable digital meters. Stationary digital meters are fixed installations, often used in residential and commercial settings for continuous monitoring of electricity, water, or gas consumption. They provide real-time data and are typically connected to a central management system. Portable digital meters, on the other hand, are handheld devices designed for temporary measurements in various environments, such as field inspections or testing. These meters offer versatility and ease of use for immediate data collection.</p></p>
