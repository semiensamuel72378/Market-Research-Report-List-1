<p><p>The snow melting agent market is divided into two primary types: liquid and solid snow melting agents. Liquid snow melting agents, typically based on calcium chloride or magnesium chloride, provide quick absorption and faster melting, making them ideal for rapid response in harsh weather conditions. In contrast, solid snow melting agents, such as rock salt or ice pellets, offer a slower but more prolonged melting effect, often preferred for larger areas or prolonged snow cover. Both types cater to diverse application needs.</p></p>
