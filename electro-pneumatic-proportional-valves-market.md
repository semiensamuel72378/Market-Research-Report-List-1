<p><p>The electro-pneumatic proportional valves market encompasses two primary types: flow control and pressure control. Flow control valves regulate the rate of airflow in pneumatic systems, ensuring precise movement and speed of actuators. In contrast, pressure control valves maintain and adjust the pressure within a system, preventing over-pressurization and ensuring safety. Both types utilize electrical signals to achieve proportional adjustments, allowing for enhanced performance and automation in various industrial applications, including manufacturing, robotics, and process control systems.</p></p>
