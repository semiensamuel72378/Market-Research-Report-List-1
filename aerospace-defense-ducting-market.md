<p><p>The Aerospace & Defense Ducting Market comprises three main types: Rigid, Semi-Rigid, and Flexible ducting. Rigid ducting, made from materials like aluminum or composites, offers high durability and is used in high-pressure systems. Semi-Rigid ducting provides a balance between flexibility and strength, ideal for moderate-pressure applications. Flexible ducting, constructed from lightweight, pliable materials, is used in areas requiring adaptability and vibration absorption. Each type caters to specific operational needs, ensuring efficient airflow, temperature control, and system reliability in aerospace and defense applications.</p></p>
