<p><p>The constipation treatment drug market comprises several types, each targeting different mechanisms. Chloride channel activators enhance intestinal fluid secretion. 5-HT4 receptor agonists stimulate gut motility. GC-C agonists increase fluid secretion and intestinal transit. Peripherally acting mu-opioid receptor antagonists counteract opioid-induced constipation by blocking receptors in the gut without affecting analgesic effects. Together, these options cater to varying patient needs and underlying causes of constipation.</p></p>
