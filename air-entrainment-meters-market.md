<p><p>The Air Entrainment Meters market consists of two main types: Water Column Type and Pressure Gauge Type. Water Column Type meters utilize a column of water to measure air entrainment by observing the displacement caused by air bubbles. In contrast, Pressure Gauge Type meters measure the pressure differential created by entrained air, providing insights into air content in fluids. Both types are essential in fields such as construction and civil engineering, where precise measurement of air content in concrete is crucial for material performance.</p></p>
