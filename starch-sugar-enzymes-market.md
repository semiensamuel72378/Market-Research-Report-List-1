<p><p>The starch and sugar enzymes market encompasses various enzyme types crucial for carbohydrate processing. Beta amylase further degrades starch into maltose, primarily used in brewing and baking. Other enzymes in this category may include glucoamylase, which converts starch into glucose, and pullulanase, which aids in the hydrolysis of starch. Together, these enzymes support diverse sectors like food, beverage, and biofuel production.</p></p>
