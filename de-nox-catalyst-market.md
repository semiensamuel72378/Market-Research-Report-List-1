<p><ul><li>Honeycomb</li><li>Flat</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The De-NOx catalyst market is primarily categorized into two types: honeycomb and flat catalysts. Honeycomb catalysts feature a cylindrical structure with numerous channels, optimizing gas flow and enhancing NOx conversion efficiency, making them ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Flat catalysts, on the other hand, have a planar design suitable for stationary sources with space limitations. Both types serve the same purpose of reducing nitrogen oxides emissions but are selected based on specific installation and performance requirements.</p></p>
