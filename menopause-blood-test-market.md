<p><p>The menopause blood test market can be categorized into fingerstick blood tests and venous blood tests. In contrast, venous blood tests involve drawing blood from a vein, offering more comprehensive and accurate hormone level assessments. Both types serve distinct needs, catering to varying preferences for testing methods while providing critical insights into menopausal health.</p></p>
