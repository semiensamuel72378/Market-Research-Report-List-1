<p><ul><li>Perfusion Disposables</li><li>Catheters</li><li>Heart Lung Machine</li><li>Breathing Heart Surgery</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cardiac surgery devices market encompasses various types designed to support heart procedures. Perfusion disposables are critical in maintaining blood flow during surgery. Catheters assist in diagnostics and treatments by facilitating access to the heart and vascular systems. The heart-lung machine temporarily takes over the heart and lung functions during surgery, ensuring oxygenation and circulation. Meanwhile, breathing heart surgery tools enable procedures on a beating heart, improving patient outcomes by reducing recovery times and complications. Together, these devices enhance the safety and efficacy of cardiac surgeries.</p></p>
