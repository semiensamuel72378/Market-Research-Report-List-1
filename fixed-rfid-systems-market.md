<p><strong>The Fixed RFID Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LF RFID System</li><li>HF RFID System</li><li>UHF RFID System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed RFID systems market consists of various types based on operational frequency. LF RFID systems operate at low frequencies (125-134 kHz), ideal for short-range applications like livestock tracking. HF RFID systems work in a frequency range of 13.56 MHz, suitable for access control and smart cards. UHF RFID systems, ranging from 300 MHz to 960 MHz, enable longer read distances and faster data transfer, commonly used in supply chain and inventory management. "Others" includes specialized or emerging RFID technologies not fitting into these categories.</p></p>
