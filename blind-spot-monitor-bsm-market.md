<p><p>A Blind Spot Monitor (BSM) is an advanced driver-assistance system (ADAS) designed to enhance vehicle safety by detecting and alerting drivers to vehicles or objects in their blind spots. Typically using sensors, cameras, or radar, BSM systems provide visual, audible, or haptic warnings to prevent potential collisions during lane changes or maneuvers. This technology has become increasingly popular in modern vehicles, contributing to improved road safety and reduced accidents.</p><p>The Blind Spot Monitor (BSM) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.4% during the forecast period. This growth is driven by rising consumer demand for advanced safety features, stringent government regulations promoting vehicle safety, and increasing adoption of ADAS technologies in both passenger and commercial vehicles. Additionally, the integration of BSM with other ADAS features, such as lane-keeping assist and adaptive cruise control, is further propelling market expansion.</p><p>Recent trends in the BSM market include the development of more sophisticated sensor technologies, such as LiDAR and AI-based systems, to improve detection accuracy and reduce false alerts. The growing popularity of electric and autonomous vehicles is also creating new opportunities for BSM integration. Furthermore, manufacturers are focusing on cost-effective solutions to make BSM systems accessible to mid-range and entry-level vehicles, broadening their market reach.</p></p>
