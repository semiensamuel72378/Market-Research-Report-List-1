<p><ul><li>Internal Drivers</li><li>External Drivers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED driver market consists of two main types: internal and external drivers. Internal drivers are integrated within the LED fixture, providing a compact solution that simplifies installation and reduces space requirements. They are commonly used in applications where space-saving is crucial. External drivers, on the other hand, are standalone units that connect to the LED source, allowing for greater flexibility in design and installation. They are often preferred for larger systems where customizable configurations are necessary for optimal performance.</p></p>
