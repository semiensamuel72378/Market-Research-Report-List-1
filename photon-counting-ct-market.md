<p><p>Photon Counting CT (Computed Tomography) technology is categorized into two primary market types: Single Channel Counting Systems and Multichannel Counting Systems. Single Channel Counting Systems utilize one detector to capture photon counts, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for specific applications. In contrast, Multichannel Counting Systems feature multiple detectors, enabling simultaneous data collection from various angles, enhancing image resolution and speed. The choice between these systems depends on the desired imaging quality and application requirements within the medical imaging field.</p></p>
