<p><ul><li>Single Volute Twin Screw Pump</li><li>Double Volutes Twin Screw Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The non-sealed twin-screw pump market includes two main types: single volute and double volute twin-screw pumps. Single volute twin-screw pumps feature a single spiral flow path, offering simplicity and efficiency for lower flow rates. In contrast, double volute twin-screw pumps have two volute chambers, enhancing balancing and reducing radial forces, which makes them suitable for higher flow applications. Both types are used in various industries for their ability to handle viscous fluids and provide reliable, steady flow.</p></p>
