<p><strong>The Industrial Refractory Alumina Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Calcined Alumina</li><li>Fused Alumina</li><li>Tabular Alumina</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial refractory alumina market consists of several types, including calcined alumina, fused alumina, and tabular alumina. Calcined alumina is produced through the calcination of aluminum hydroxide and is used for its high melting point and chemical stability. Fused alumina, created by melting bauxite under electric arc, exhibits exceptional hardness and is utilized in abrasive applications. Tabular alumina is produced by sintering alumina at high temperatures, resulting in high strength and thermal shock resistance. Other types comprise various specialized alumina forms for niche applications.</p></p>
