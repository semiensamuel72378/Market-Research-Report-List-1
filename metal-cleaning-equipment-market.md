<p><ul><li>Solvent Metal Cleaning Equipment</li><li>Aqueous Metal Cleaning Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal cleaning equipment market is primarily divided into two types: solvent metal cleaning equipment and aqueous metal cleaning equipment. Solvent metal cleaning equipment uses chemical solvents to effectively remove contaminants like grease and oil from metal surfaces, typically offering fast and efficient cleaning. In contrast, aqueous metal cleaning equipment relies on water-based solutions combined with detergents or surfactants, making it more environmentally friendly. This type is often utilized for more delicate cleaning tasks and offers reduced solvent emissions.</p></p>
